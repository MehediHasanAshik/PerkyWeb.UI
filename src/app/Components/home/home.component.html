DataBase Information

Three Tables:
Employees(EmployeeId, firstName, lastName, dateOfBirth, departmentId, positionId)

Departments(departmentId, departmentName)

Positions(positionId, Title)

Employee table is referenced by foreign key in Department and Position table

So, while inserting into employees, we have to insert only those departmentId and positionId whose are available on those table
